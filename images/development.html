Bostrad engages in managing the implementation of development projects for land, air and maritime sector. We evaluate the effectiveness of coordination and supervision of the implementation of the project as well as coordinate, assist government agencies and private institutions or project owners to achieve their short term or long term project goals.

Land

Bostrad Supply Chain in collaboration with its land development experts works to develop land and ensure the appropriate use of it for developmental purposes.

In agriculture, our Farming experts works with local farmers to develop their arable lands to yield to itâ€™s optimum and full potential.

Recommendation, supply and application of organic and biodegradable pesticides and fertilizers are part of the core consultancy and developmental services we render.

In Mining, our investors work in tandem with professional miners to extract natural resources lands are innately impregnated with and ensure the appropriate usage of it. Rail, road, housing and other landed constructions falls under this category.

Air

Under the air sector, we focus primarily on airports with the main purpose of upgrading the infrastructure in order to enhance the capabilities of existing terminals.

Part of our services also include facility management, most especially in the area of pest control and installation of anti-birdstrike devices.

Overall, we implement and monitor aviation projects by ensuring proper planning and optimisation of expenditure are in place by the Aviation Ministry.

Sea (Maritime)

We engage alongside our technical partners in the construction of sea ports, dredging for cruise safety, and as well as supply and rental of dredging machineries.

Construction of bridges across seas, rivers is also a part of the developmental services we render in this sector.
