Wheat, The King of Cereals

Wheat is one of the world's most commonly consumed cereal grains. It comes from a type of grass (Triticum) that is grown in countless varieties worldwide.

Bostrad has emerged as a reliable Exporter and Supplier of Wheat Grains worldwide. The company offers the very best quality Wheat to the clients at market leading prices. Wheat, being a commonly consumed item across the world, is widely demanded and the company caters to the demands with great efficiency. Sourced directly from farmers, these grains are known for purity and excellent smell attributes. Moreover, we make the supply of these grains to customers at equitable price tags.

We supply a large variety of wheat such as Bread wheat, durum, spelt, emmer, einkorn, and Khorasan wheat.
