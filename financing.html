In 2016, Bostrad Supply Chain commenced financing and developing projects of significant size with the aim of providing jobs and building infrastructure in less developed countries.

In a bid to ensure that professionalism forms the bedrock of this new concept, Bostrad Supply Chain developed business synergy with prime international project investors in order to fund projects with infrastructure and humanitarian benefits.

We will create at least, 10, 000 jobs and entrepreneurs by the year 2022.

Funding of projects are on qualification and eligibility basis but are not limited to agriculture, infrastructure, construction, technology and development in general.

We will also educate beneficiaries on the growing importance of the use technology in today's businesses.

We are committed towards driving economic growth through the fostering of entrepreneurship especially among youths in developing countries.
